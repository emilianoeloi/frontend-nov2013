Durham County Cricket Club

Durham County Cricket Club was established on 23rd May 1882, and they played their first match at Chester Road cricket ground, Sunderland, on 12th June 1882 when they defeated Northumberland by 4 wickets.The Club won its first piece of silverware in 2007 after the Dynamos beat Hampshire Hawks at Lord's in the Friends Provident Trophy final. In 2008 Durham County Cricket Club won the LV County Championship and the Second Eleven Championship, both for the first time in the Club's history. The first eleven went on to retain the Championship title in 2009.
